The Experience

I designed the logo for polizei-gewalt.com. Polizeigewalt is a german word which means police violence. To me the name of the blog raised a very important question that the logo needed to answer - 'what about police violence?' The blog was against police violence so it felt right to put a hand signifying 'stop' in front of polizei-gewalt.com making the brand's mission loud and clear.
